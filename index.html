Dhaval Joshi

Gold Coast, Queensland, Australia

I arrived in Australia in 2024 to pursue a Master of International Tourism & Hospitality Management at Griffith University — and found myself working the floors of some of the Gold Coast's finest hotels along the way.

This journal is where I document what the hospitality industry looks like from the inside: the quiet craft of a perfectly turned-down room, the guest moments that stay with you, and the standards that separate good service from truly great service.
